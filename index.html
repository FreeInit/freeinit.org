FreeInit.org is the community initiative for free and open-source alternatives to the X Desktop Group's systemd init system and middleware suite.

Our first goal is to create a listing of the software and libraries provided by systemd, enumerating the free and open-source alternatives to each of these, with notes on the status of these projects and the level of compatibility with any systemd interfaces.

We hope to facilitate the cooperation and coordination of all projects aiming at providing free alternatives to systemd components.

If you feel you can add to our project, please visit our GitHub repository and create an issue.
